

PROJECT TITLE: Snake (a simplified version)
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: CS2 Project, and for fun!
VERSION or DATE: Version 3.
HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: Open up "project.greenfoot". Then press "Run" to begin
AUTHORS: Jack Komaroff
USER INSTRUCTIONS:
Control the snake with the arrow keys. If your score is more than 5, you can press shift for a quick boost. But be carful, if you have much more than 5, your boost could be massive! Try to eat as many mushrooms to increase your score without hitting the edge of the garden. If you lose, just click "Reset" and try again.
